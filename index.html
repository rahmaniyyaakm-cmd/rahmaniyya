Welcome to Our School

Rahmaniyya Public School is a premier educational institution committed to providing quality education with a focus on holistic development. Our dynamic curriculum and dedicated faculty ensure that every child reaches their full potential.

School Profile

Established in 1995, Rahmaniyya Public School has been a beacon of knowledge in the region. We offer a supportive environment where students are encouraged to explore their interests and excel in both academics and co-curricular activities.

Vision & Mission

🎯 Our Vision

To be a world-class center of learning that develops responsible, innovative, and compassionate global citizens.

🚀 Our Mission

To provide a safe and stimulating learning environment that empowers students to achieve academic excellence and personal growth.

Milestones

  • 🏆 25+ years of academic excellence.
  • 🎓 Over 2,000 successful alumni across the globe.
  • 🏅 Multiple district and state-level awards in sports and arts.
  • 💻 Integrated modern IT labs and smart classrooms.

LKG (Lower Kindergarten)

Our LKG program focuses on foundational skills: Alphabets, numbers, colors, and creative drawing through play-way methods.

UKG (Upper Kindergarten)

Preparation for primary schooling with basic English literacy, mathematical concepts, and social skills.

Class I

Core subjects: English, Mathematics, and Environmental Studies (EVS) with a focus on conceptual clarity.

Class II

Continuing the journey with advanced English, Maths, and EVS modules.

Class III

Introduction to formal Science and deeper linguistic exploration in English and regional languages.

Class IV

Building strong analytical skills in Maths, Social Science, and Language Arts.
