School Profile

Rahmaniyya Public School, Akampadam, was established in 1995 with a vision to provide quality education in a nurturing environment. We focus on holistic development, combining academic rigour with character building.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To empower students with knowledge and values to become responsible global citizens.

Mission: To provide a stimulating learning environment that encourages curiosity, integrity, and excellence.

Kindergarten (LKG & UKG)

Our Kindergarten program focuses on the foundational skills of language, literacy, and numeracy through play-based learning and creative expression. We nurture the natural curiosity of every child.

Primary Classes (I - IV)

In our Primary section, we follow a comprehensive curriculum that emphasizes core subjects while integrating arts, sports, and value education. Our goal is to build strong academic foundations and critical thinking skills.
